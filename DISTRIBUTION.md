- MOBILE — after 11 PM IST (~20-25 min, spread). Note: 11 PM IST ≈ 1:30 PM US Eastern / 10:30 AM US Pacific = prime US engagement time. All social/distribution that benefits from a live US audience goes here.

## Rule (hard-won)
ONE new active channel at a time. Reddit is it. Twitter stays light (already running). HN/Dev.to/SEO are background or weekend tasks, not parallel growth pushes. Do not spread thin — that failure mode already cost a week.

## The real success signal
Traffic is raw material. The unproven conversion is traffic-to-RELATIONSHIP. Watch organic subscriber count and unprompted shares/replies as the true leading indicator of monetizability. 100 engaged subscribers matters more than 1,000 visitors. Decision gates: geography ≥25% US/EU by early Aug, Twitter referrers by 3 Aug, conversion test (form now more visible) over next 2-3 weeks.
